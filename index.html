Why it's different

Plenty of software will sell you "AI for landlords." The point here isn't the AI — it's that the work actually gets done, safely, and stays legal.

  • A service, not a tool. It runs the lifecycle for you, rather than being one more app you have to drive.
  • Kept legal as the rules change. UK letting law keeps moving — the system's compliance rules are maintained centrally and updated for everyone.
  • You stay in control. Anything that commits money, sends a legal document, or speaks to a tenant waits for your approval.
  • Your data stays yours. Tenant details and any keys live on your own machine — they never leave it.
